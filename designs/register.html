Register for Greenway Walk

All members of your walking party must be registered on the same booking form.
We need an email to send your booking confirmation and security code. We will also use it to send a reminder just before the event.
We need a contact phone number for on the day, in case you are late back or there is an issue.

GDPR & Privacy use statement.
Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is recognised as email addresses, phone numbers & participant names.

Email addresses & telephone numbers will purged within 10 days of the event. A secure signature of the email address will be persisted for statistical purposes to allow repeat sign ups to be monitored over multiple years. Email addresses will only be used to send transactional notifications, but will not be used for any other purpose.

Participant names will be shared with section leaders prior, during and following the event. Section leaders are granted legitimate purpose due to badge requirement validations, invoicing support and as part of the Scout Association (TSA) membership. Participant names will be retained for leader reference.

Any transmission or sharing (as detailed above) of submitted information will be done over encrypted connections, with data access being secured to only relevant sections for the TSA member viewing the data.

Registering for the walk, grants the use of personal data in accordance with the above stated usage, in line with the above timelines.
